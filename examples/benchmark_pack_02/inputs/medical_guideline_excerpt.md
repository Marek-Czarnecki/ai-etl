# Northwind Community Clinic
## Medication Safety Excerpt (Synthetic)

This excerpt is part of a training-only clinical operations manual for the Northwind Community Clinic. It describes internal safety checks for a fictional formulary and is not medical advice.

### Section 1. General order documentation
1. Medication orders must include a documented clinical indication and the ordering clinician role.
2. If an exception is required, the exception must be documented in the order notes.

### Section 2. Nevarel (fictional anticoagulant)
3. Do not order Nevarel for patients with a documented Nevarel allergy.
4. Do not order Nevarel when the most recent eGFR is below 30 mL/min/1.73m2.
5. If Nevarel dose is above 200 mg, hemoglobin must be rechecked within 48 hours.
6. Nevarel must not be ordered for pregnant patients unless an exception is documented.

### Section 3. CardioFlux (fictional antiarrhythmic)
7. Before CardioFlux doses above 50 mg, potassium must be 5.5 mmol/L or lower.

### Section 4. VasoLift (fictional vasopressor)
8. VasoLift infusion must not be started if systolic blood pressure is below 90 mmHg.

### Section 5. Zyphra (fictional antibiotic)
9. Zyphra orders require the indication field to be completed.

End of excerpt.
